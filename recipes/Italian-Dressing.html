Italian-Dressing

Description

So you are looking for a recipe for an original italian salad dressing? You are at the right place.

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese, finely grated
  • 1 Tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 garlic clove, finely grated or pressed
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 3/4 tsp fine sea salt, or added to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper, or to taste

    • Directions

    1. Step
      2. Combine all ingredients in a mason jar, cover tightly with lid and shake until well combined and emulsified.
    2. Step
      3. Add more salt to taste if desired. Use right away or refrigerate up to 2 weeks.

    Enjoy your meal!
